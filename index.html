Music is an art form and cultural activity that uses sound to express emotions, ideas, and stories. It typically involves elements like melody, harmony, rhythm, and timbre, can be performed using the voice, instruments, or electronic devices. Music spans a wide range of genres, from classical and jazz to rock, hip-hop, electronic, and beyond. It plays a significant role in entertainment, rituals, social movements, and personal expression. Music has tha power to influence emotions, bring people together, and reflect the diversity of human experience.
